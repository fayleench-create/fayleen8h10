Profile

Ashlyn Banner is a 16 year old girl aswell as the protagonist of the webcomic of School Bus Graveyard and the group leader of their little friendgroup. Ashlyn is an introverted girl who finds interacting with other people exhausting. But after she got forced into the phantom dimension with some of her group members, she eventually accepted them as friends or maybe even fond family. Every time the clock hits 12 AM, her and her friendgroup are forced into an apocolypse with creepy tall black figures on the hunt for them. She's the type to take responsibility even if it wasn't her fault.
